FOOTBALL UNIFORMS

Barcelona's
first form.
The initial design was simple - the T-shirt was divided in half vertically in blue and garnet colors. In 1910, Barcelona officially adopted the 3-stripe T-shirt design. It happened when the Catalans started competing against Basque and French teams in the rather prestigious Pyrenees Cup and won it 4 years in a row. For the first 10 years, Catalans performed in white shorts.
